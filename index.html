ABOUT THE GAME

❮ ❯

Earth's Guardians is an immersive educational adventure that invites players to embark on a mission to restore our planet's health while exploring the wonders of science and environmental stewardship. Set in a world grappling with severe ecological degradation, players assume the role of a young environmentalist, guided by a wise mentor, as they navigate through a series of engaging quests rooted in the GLOBE Program protocols.

Key Features:

Dynamic Storytelling: Experience an evolving narrative that combines personal stories with global challenges.
Multiple Missions: Tackle real-world environmental issues.
Engaging Side Quests: Participate in community-driven projects.
Impactful Choices: Make decisions that influence the game's outcome.
Transition to Space: Choose to become an astronaut or a researcher.
